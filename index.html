Global Renewable Trends

The global energy transition from historical use of fossil fuel based electrical generation to renewable
generation has increased in pace over the past decade with the advancement in technology, access to capital,
and climate change affecting all life in real time. As a potential investor in future solar and wind
generation projects we want to understand/analyze global trends in electrical production by solar energy,
wind energy, and the investment in these generation capacities.
